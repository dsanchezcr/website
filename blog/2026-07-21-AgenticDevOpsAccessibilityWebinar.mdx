## What the session covers

This webinar focuses on a point that teams cannot afford to miss: faster delivery with AI and agents is only progress if the software stays inclusive for real people.

- Why accessibility has to remain part of the delivery baseline, not a late review step.
- How AI assistants and agents can help teams move faster with documentation, testing, and implementation work, while still requiring human judgment.
- Practical ways to keep inclusive design, quality standards, and delivery workflows aligned instead of treating them as separate concerns.

## Why this topic matters

Agentic workflows can accelerate software delivery, but speed alone does not make a product better. If accessibility gets skipped, deferred, or treated as optional, teams simply ship exclusion faster. Inclusive delivery means building systems, reviews, and engineering habits that keep accessibility visible from the start.

That is the core idea behind this session. AI can help teams do more, but it should raise the quality bar, not lower it.
