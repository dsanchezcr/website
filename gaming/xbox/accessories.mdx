# 🎛️ Xbox Accessories Setup

I like building immersive setups for flight and racing games on Xbox and PC. This page covers the accessories I currently use and the games I play with them.

## ✈️ Flight Setup

- **Thrustmaster T-Flight Hotas One** for a classic joystick + throttle setup
- **Logitech G PRO Flight Yoke System with Rudder Pedals** for a more realistic civil aviation experience

## 🏎️ Racing Setup

- **Logitech G920** wheel + pedals
- **Logitech Driving Force Shifter**
- Mounted on a **GT Omega Apex Racing Wheel Stand** for stability and comfort

## 🎮 Games I Play with These Accessories

- **Microsoft Flight Simulator**
- **Ace Combat**
- **Chorus**
- **Forza Horizon**
- **SnowRunner**

I am also exploring more games that support these accessories, especially through Game Pass and new releases.
