## 🎮 Xbox Full Screen Experience (Handheld Mode)

On the gaming SSD, I boot directly into the **Xbox full screen experience**, the same console-like, controller-first interface you find on handhelds like the ROG Ally. It turns my desktop into a big-screen console: no desktop clutter, no mouse required, just power on and play.

I enable it with the **[Xbox Full Screen Experience Tool](https://github.com/8bit2qubit/XboxFullscreenExperienceTool)** by 8bit2qubit, a one-click GUI utility that safely turns on the hidden Xbox full screen gaming experience on Windows 11.

:::info[Why I love handheld mode on a desktop]
- **Console-like boot** straight into a controller-friendly UI
- **Handheld experience** to navigate my whole library with a controller
- **Less overhead** than the full Windows desktop while gaming
:::
