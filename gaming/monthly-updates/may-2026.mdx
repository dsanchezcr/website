# May 2026 Gaming Update

![May 2026 Gaming Update](https://dsanchezcrwebsite.blob.core.windows.net/images/gaming/monthly-updates/may-26-gaming.jpg)

May is shaping up to be one of the biggest months of the year, headlined by Forza Horizon 6 finally taking us to Japan and IO Interactive's 007 First Light. Here's my personal roundup of what's happening this month.

## Upcoming Releases

Here are the May 2026 releases I'm most excited about:

### Directive 8020 — May 12
Sci-fi action game set on a distant planet. After years of anticipation since its initial reveal, seeing this one finally launch is exciting, Season 2 Premiere The Dark Pictures Anthology. Coming to **PC, PS5, and Xbox Series X|S**.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="61OMILUgVyo" title="Directive 8020" />

### Forza Horizon 6 — May 19 (Early Access May 15)
The long-awaited Japan setting is here. Playground Games is delivering the largest and most dense map in Horizon history, with over 550 real-world cars, Tokyo City as the biggest urban area in the series, Touge Battles on mountain passes, and customizable garages. Available on **Xbox Series X|S and PC** (day one with Game Pass). PS5 version coming later in 2026.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="pWw-UENvdTw" title="Forza Horizon 6" />

### Yoshi and the Mysterious Book — May 21
A new Yoshi adventure exclusive to **Nintendo Switch 2**. Nintendo keeps delivering strong first-party titles for the new console.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="tdOu4yG8ntc" title="Yoshi and the Mysterious Book" />

### LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight — May 22
A new LEGO Batman game that brings the brick-building charm to Gotham. Available on **PC, PS5, and Xbox Series X|S**.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="j5ha2VwHJCw" title="LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight" />

### 007 First Light — May 27
IO Interactive's reimagined James Bond origin story. Players step into the shoes of a young, 26-year-old Bond as he earns his 00 status. The Hitman studio is blending stealth, action, and cinematic set-pieces with combat inspired by the Arkham games. Available on **PC, Nintendo Switch 2, PS5, and Xbox Series X|S**.
