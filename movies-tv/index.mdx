## My Favorite Theaters

### 🎭 AMC DINE-IN Disney Springs 24

My go-to theater in Orlando, Florida. As an **AMC A-List** subscriber, I get to enjoy:

- **Up to 3 movies per week** at any AMC theater
- Access to **IMAX, Dolby Cinema, and premium formats** at no extra cost
- **Reserved seating** and advance ticket booking
- The **DINE-IN experience** — enjoy a full meal while watching

AMC Disney Springs 24 is conveniently located at Disney Springs, making it the perfect spot to combine a movie night with dining and shopping.

### 🎭 Cinépolis Costa Rica

When I'm in Costa Rica, **Cinépolis** is my theater of choice. Known for its comfortable seating, great sound systems, and an excellent movie selection across multiple locations in the country.
