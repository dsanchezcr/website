My go-to theater in Orlando. As an **AMC A-List** subscriber, I get to enjoy:

- **Up to 4 movies per week** at any AMC theater
- Access to **Dolby Cinema** at no extra cost (my favorite format for the best sound and picture quality)

When I'm in Costa Rica, **Cinépolis** is my theater of choice.
